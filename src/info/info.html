About Us

We are a group of dedicated and passionate rising senior students at the University of Massachusetts Lowell, all majoring in Computer Science. This project is a result of our efforts in the GUI II (Graphical User Interface) class, where we have combined our technical skills and creativity to bring you an innovative and user-friendly financial management solution.


Our Mission

Our mission is to provide user-friendly tools and resources that enable better financial management, ensuring a secure and prosperous future for our users.
